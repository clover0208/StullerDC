How We Care

In order to allow more patients to receive better diagnosis, bring patients closer to medical resources, we created this online community platform

1

Post/Search post in community

Post your symptom in the community so that whenever experienced patients see your symptoms they can respond to you. The answer will also benefit other patients with the same symptoms.

2

Expertise for answer

Our platform is monitored by experienced and responsible expertise in rare diseases. They are patiently helping you for free.

3

Medical Resource

Patients will share information concerning reliable doctors and hospitals in different cities, saving you time and money for efficient treatment.

4

Online video consultation

We arrange free online doctor consultation sections for you to join. Have suggestion to better find treatment.

5

Mutual Help

To build a good community, we also hope you can share feedback concerning doctors and hospitals in the post forum to benefit other patients as well.
